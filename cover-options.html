PROFESSOR ANTONY LONG EMBARKS UPON HIS SIXTIETH YEAR

It has come to the attention of this publication that Professor Antony Long — esteemed son of Dulwich College, veteran of the Greenland ice sheet, rescuer of stricken colleagues in Spitsbergen, and Vice Chancellor of Durham University — has this day commenced his sixtieth year upon the globe.

Born but twenty minutes after his elder twin, Professor Long displayed ambition from the earliest age. Like the illustrious Ernest Shackleton, likewise of Dulwich College, he has long favoured the Polar regions over more temperate climates.

This special edition records, for posterity, the life and achievements of a man of uncommon fortitude — a Dulwich man, in the finest tradition of the College.
